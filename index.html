About Me

Welcome to my webpage! My name is Valeria Bodishtianu, I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Economics Department at Cornell University.

My primary research interests are Information Economics, Networks and Artificial Intelligence. My current works utilise economic theory, computational economic models, behavioral experiments, and AI technology to further our understanding of the driving forces behind increasing ideological divide and spread of misinformation in digital spaces. My research aims both to answer the question of the unerlying social dynamics of online behavior, as well as to explore the ways these models can be leveraged to create new policies and platform tools.
