About Me

Welcome to my webpage! My name is Valeria Bodishtianu, I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Economics Department at Cornell University.

My primary research interests are game theory, social networks, agent-based modelling and LLM-based agents. My current works utilise economic theory, experiments, computational economic models and LLM-powered generative agents to further our understanding of the driving forces behind ideological polarization and spread of misinformation online.
